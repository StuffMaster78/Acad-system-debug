## üìù Creating Orders

### Step 1: Access Order Creation

1. Click **"Create Order"** or **"Place Order"** button
2. You'll see the order creation form

### Step 2: Fill Order Details

#### Basic Information
- **Topic/Title** - Enter the topic or title of your paper
- **Paper Type** - Select from dropdown (Essay, Research Paper, etc.)
- **Academic Level** - Select your level (High School, Undergraduate, etc.)
- **Number of Pages** - Enter the required number of pages
- **Deadline** - Select your deadline date and time

#### Additional Details
- **Subject** - Select the subject area
- **Formatting Style** - Choose citation style (APA, MLA, etc.)
- **English Type** - Select English variant (US, UK, etc.)
- **Type of Work** - Select type (Writing, Editing, etc.)

#### Instructions
- **Order Instructions** - Provide detailed instructions for the writer
  - Include specific requirements
  - Mention key points to cover
  - Specify any sources or materials to use
  - Add formatting preferences

### Step 3: Review and Submit

1. **Review Order Summary**
   - Check all details are correct
   - Review pricing breakdown
   - Verify deadline

2. **Apply Discount Code** (Optional)
   - Enter discount code if you have one
   - Click "Apply" to see updated price

3. **Submit Order**
   - Click **"Create Order"** button
   - You'll be redirected to payment page

### Order Creation Tips

‚úÖ **Do:**
- Provide detailed instructions
- Set realistic deadlines
- Include all requirements upfront
- Attach reference materials if needed

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Leave instructions vague
- Set unrealistic deadlines
- Forget to mention important details
- Skip the review step

## üìä Managing Orders

### Viewing Your Orders

1. **Access Orders Page**
   - Click **"Orders"** in the navigation menu
   - You'll see a list of all your orders

2. **Order List Features**
   - **Search** - Search by order ID, topic, or status
   - **Filter** - Filter by status, date, payment status
   - **Sort** - Sort by date, status, or price

### Order Statuses

- **Pending** - Order created, awaiting payment
- **Unpaid** - Payment not yet completed
- **In Progress** - Writer is working on your order
- **On Hold** - Order temporarily paused
- **Submitted** - Writer has submitted the work
- **Under Editing** - Editor is reviewing the work
- **Completed** - Order is finished and ready
- **Revision Requested** - You requested changes
- **On Revision** - Writer is making revisions
- **Disputed** - Order has a dispute

### Order Actions

#### View Order Details
- Click on any order to view full details
- See progress, messages, files, and timeline

#### Request Revision
1. Open the order
2. Click **"Request Revision"**
3. Provide revision instructions
4. Submit request

#### Download Files
1. Open the order
2. Go to **"Files"** section
3. Click **"Download"** on any file

#### Rate and Review
1. After order completion
2. Click **"Rate Order"**
3. Provide rating and feedback
4. Submit review

2. **Select Payment Method**

- Check **"Payment Reminders"** in dashboard
- View reminder history and preferences

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Can't Create Order
- **Check**: Are all required fields filled?
- **Check**: Is your account verified?
- **Solution**: Contact support if issue persists

#### Payment Failed
- **Check**: Payment method details correct?
- **Check**: Sufficient funds available?
- **Solution**: Try different payment method

#### Can't Access Order
- **Check**: Are you logged in?
- **Check**: Is order assigned to your account?
- **Solution**: Refresh page or contact support

#### Writer Not Responding
- **Check**: Check message status
- **Check**: Order status (may be on hold)
- **Solution**: Contact support for assistance

## ‚ùì FAQ

### Order Management

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**  
A: Orders can be cancelled before payment. After payment, contact support.

**Q: How do I request a revision?**  
A: Open the order, click "Request Revision", provide instructions, and submit.

**Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the work?**  
A: Request a revision or open a dispute through the order page.

**Q: Can I extend the deadline?**  
A: Yes, you can request a deadline extension. Contact support or use the order page.

### Payment

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**  
A: Credit/Debit cards, PayPal, Bank Transfer, and Wallet.

**Q: Is payment secure?**  
A: Yes, all payments are processed through secure payment gateways.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Refunds are available in certain circumstances. Contact support for details.

**Q: How do I add funds to my wallet?**  
A: Go to Payments ‚Üí Wallet ‚Üí Add Funds, enter amount, and complete payment.

### Communication

**Q: How do I contact my writer?**  
A: Use the Messages tab in the order page or the Messages section in navigation.

**Q: How quickly do writers respond?**  
A: Response times vary, but most respond within 24 hours.

**Q: Can I attach files to messages?**  
A: Yes, you can attach files when sending messages.

### Account

**Q: How do I update my profile?**  
A: Go to Profile in navigation, make changes, and click Save.

**Q: Can I change my email?**  
A: Contact support to change your email address.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**  
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page, enter email, and follow instructions.
