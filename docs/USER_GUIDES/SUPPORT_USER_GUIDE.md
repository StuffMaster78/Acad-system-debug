## üöÄ Getting Started

### First Login

1. **Access the System**
   - Navigate to the support portal
   - Click "Login" in the top right corner

2. **Login Credentials**
   - Enter your email address

3. **Dashboard Access**

## üé´ Ticket Management

### Viewing Tickets

1. **Access Tickets**
   - Go to **"Tickets"** in navigation
   - See all assigned tickets

2. **Ticket Queue**
   - **My Tickets** - Tickets assigned to you
   - **Open Tickets** - All open tickets
   - **Pending** - Awaiting response
   - **In Progress** - Being worked on

### Ticket Actions

**Take Ticket**
1. View available tickets
2. Click **"Take Ticket"**
3. Ticket assigned to you

**Respond to Ticket**
1. Open the ticket
2. Type your response
3. Attach files if needed
4. Click **"Send"**

**Close Ticket**
1. Open the ticket
2. Resolve the issue
3. Add closing notes
4. Click **"Close Ticket"**

**Escalate Ticket**
1. Open the ticket
2. Click **"Escalate"**
3. Select escalation reason
4. Submit escalation
